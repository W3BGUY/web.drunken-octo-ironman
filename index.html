LevelOne understands the importance and challenge of designing, implementing and successfully completing a network infrastructure project. We understand the importance of the planning, communication, and documentation that keeps a successful project on schedule.

Planning

Every project begins with the need to evaluate and analyze customer specific current and future requirements.  LevelOne project managers work with our customers to formulate the best design using the latest industry information and a wide variety of viable options.  The planning stage also allows for developing timelines and milestones to ensure projects are completed within budget and time allowed.

Implementation

Successful implementation of a structured cabling system begins with certified installation experts. Our technicians are trained and certified through several major structured cabling manufacturers. Our experienced and knowledgeable staff ensures that your infrastructure is installed, tested, and documented according to the most recent industry standards.

Maintenance

Once a project is completed, we do not consider it an end to the relationship with our customer. LevelOne will provide scheduled or on call maintenance services for your Moves, Adds and Changes (MAC).  Additionally, LevelOne will cross train your in-house technicians to perform minor maintenance procedures that will not void manufacturer warranties.

Satisfaction

Long term customer satisfaction and quality installations are the driving factor in the way LevelOne conducts business. LevelOne subscribes to the philosophy that every customer is a partner. Every installation must meet or exceed customer expectation.
